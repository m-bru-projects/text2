# The Thaw - Puzzle Graph

## Design Goal

The puzzles are civic rituals turned back into machinery. The player should not feel they are collecting arbitrary keys. Each obstruction exists because Veyr Station was designed by a culture that fused law, heat, bells, and memory.

## Core Graph

Start: Tram Vestibule

Parallel discovery layer:

- Examine the bell poster, oath plaque, and frost-printer in the Petition Hall to learn the Covenant's public language.
- Speak with Brother Caldus in the Chapel of Gauges to learn that bells are locks, not ornaments.
- Speak with Ione Glass in the Rookery to learn that the Salt Choir uses contraband frequencies and distrusts the official archive.
- Find the ration token in the Infirmary to learn how legal heat was denied to named people.

Gate A: Petition Hall to Chapel / Rookery is open.

Gate B: Archive Stacks

- Required world knowledge: the Founding Toll.
- Mechanical action: say or recite "four one seven two" at the choir grille in the Chapel of Gauges.
- Result: the bell lock wakes and opens the Archive Stacks. This is clued by Caldus, the bell poster, and repeated 4-1-7-2 motifs.

Gate C: Under-ice Garden

- Required item: brass tooth from the Infirmary.
- Required action: use brass tooth with nursery automaton in the Archive Stacks.
- Result: the automaton finishes its old civic lesson, releases the garden latch, and gives the player the Soft Ledger.

Parallel midgame:

- In the Archive Stacks, read the Soft Ledger and Kest marginalia to learn that the Tide Engine was intentionally limited by legal governors.
- In the Engine Nave, talk to Pavel and examine the pressure chart to learn the true sequence for rebalancing: floodgate, return, witness.
- In the Under-ice Garden, examine the salt font and speak with Ione to learn the Choir's transmission rite.

Gate D: Governor's Reliquary

- Required knowledge: Soft Ledger plus Kest marginalia.
- Action: show Soft Ledger to the Governor persona, or ask the Governor about the Soft Ledger after learning it exists.
- Result: the persona exposes its contradiction and unlocks the emergency mandate.

Gate E: Signal Lantern / Endgame

- Required state: engine balanced.
- Required state: emergency mandate released.
- Required item/knowledge: Soft Ledger.
- Action: send Soft Ledger on the signal lantern.
- Result: evacuation signal goes out as confession rather than simple distress. Ending records Veyr Station's rescue and indictment.

## Optional Lore Loops

- Ask Mara about her mother after reading the Soft Ledger to reveal her mother was not dead, but administratively erased.
- Ask Caldus about the Founding Toll after opening the Archive Stacks to reveal the hidden casualty meaning.
- Ask Pavel about Ruvan Kest to learn that Kest sabotaged his own engine's political override but left the repair ritual in plain sight.
- Ask Ione about the Free Melt after raising the lantern access to learn what rescue may cost.

## Macro Changes

- Opening the Archive Stacks changes station atmosphere: the air becomes paper-wet, colder, and full of awakened catalog chimes.
- Rebalancing the Tide Engine changes descriptions across the station: the floor vibration steadies, steam returns to wall pipes, and some flooded passages become passable.
- Releasing the emergency mandate changes the legal status of the signal lantern: the player can transmit the Soft Ledger as a civic act instead of smuggling.
